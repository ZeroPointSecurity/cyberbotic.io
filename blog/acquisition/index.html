Cyberbotic has been acquired

July 31, 2019 Shelley Owen

1 minute read

We are thrilled to annouce that Cyberbotic has been acquired by Zero-Point Security!

The Zero-Point Security mission statement is:

to make Red Teaming knowledge and skillsets more accessible and affordable by providing high-quality training materials and lab environments in a scalable, online format - therefore enabling businesses and industries to improve their cyber defence capabilities and adversarial resilience.

We at Cyberbotic have pioneered the development of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies, specifically applied to anti-virus and end-point protection products. Rest assured, our vision of providing quality and effective products to our customers will go unchanged.
